WHAT IS DRAGON BOAT?

Dragon boat racing is a team water sport where crews race in long, narrow boats decorated with dragon heads and tails. The gameplay involves synchronizing paddling strokes to propel the boat forward, guided by a drummer and a steerer, with the goal of finishing the race in the shortest time possible.
